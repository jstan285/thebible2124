[SYMBOLISM AND POTENTIAL INTERPRETATIONS]

“According to its kind” may symbolize cosmic order, echoing deeper metaphysical patterning.

The inclusion of wild, domestic, and creeping life reflects a totality of terrestrial motion and relation.

Divine seeing implies relational engagement, not distant observation — the divine perceives and responds.

The declaration “it was good” may point toward ontological value — inherent worth regardless of utility.
