[SYMBOLISM AND POTENTIAL INTERPRETATIONS]

“Living soul” (nefesh chayyah) applied to animals equates their aliveness with that of humans — inviting ethical reflection.

Green plant = shared sustenance — unifying earth-dwellers through diet.

“It was so” = divine intentionality; not just allowance, but enactment.

Absence of carnivory suggests original harmony or ideal.
