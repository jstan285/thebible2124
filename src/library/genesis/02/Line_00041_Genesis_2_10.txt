Interpretive Reflections
-This is a verse about expansion from center—Eden as the field-origin from which resonance and life extend. The river’s emergence is not a closed loop—it is a system of intentional diffusion. The branching into four is significant: four represents wholeness in all directions, suggesting that the Edenic field was meant to extend, to reach, to flow. This is not containment but seeding. Eden is a central harmonic node, and the river’s branches represent the multiplication of that field potential into the broader creation.
