[SYMBOLISM AND POTENTIAL INTERPRETATIONS]

“Chiddeqel” (Tigris) and “Perat” (Euphrates) are deeply embedded rivers in Near Eastern and biblical imagination.

“Eastward of Assyria” may signify movement toward origin, dawn, or sacred beginning.

Four rivers may represent wholeness, balance, or the four corners of a mapped cosmos.

[TRANSLATION RANGE SNAPSHOT]
