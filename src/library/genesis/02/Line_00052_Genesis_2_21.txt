Interpretive Reflections
-This moment is a quantum surgery of the soul. The human must be suspended—consciousness withdrawn—to allow the division of unity into relational duality. The term tzela does not imply a singular rib but likely refers to a symmetrical side—a structural component of wholeness. This is not removal for lack, but division for coherence. The resealing suggests the original human is not diminished, but restructured—the field reclosed with intention. The next phase will not be a new creation, but a drawing-forth—a derivative harmony.
