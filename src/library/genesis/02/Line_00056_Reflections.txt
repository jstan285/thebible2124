[SYMBOLISM AND POTENTIAL INTERPRETATIONS]

Nakedness as symbol of full presence — no pretense or separation.

Absence of shame marks a state beyond judgment — not ignorance, but unfragmented selfhood.

“They were not ashamed” = no projection, no internal recoil — relational harmony.

Moment framed as an archetypal ideal — unfallen mutuality.
