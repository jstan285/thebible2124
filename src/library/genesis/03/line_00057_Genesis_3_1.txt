Interpretive Reflections
-This is not a lie; it is a field reorientation. The serpent does not deny the Divine’s word—it distorts the context. The original instruction (Genesis 2:16–17) began with permission: “From every tree you may eat… except one.” The serpent inverts this frame, emphasizing restriction, not freedom. This is the essence of dissonance—not opposition, but subtle misalignment. The woman is not yet deceived—but the field is now vibrating differently. The observer's internal resonance must choose which framing to follow.
