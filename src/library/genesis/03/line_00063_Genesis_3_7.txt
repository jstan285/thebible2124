Interpretive Reflections
-This is the moment shame enters the human experience—not because something changed in their bodies, but because their perception fragmented. They now see themselves from outside. The original resonance—where transparency was relational beauty—becomes exposure and threat. And so, they begin to build barriers: clothing, walls, masks. These fig-leaf coverings are the first human-made tools in scripture. But they are not for creation or cultivation—they are for self-protection. The field is no longer unified. They are now selves in need of shielding.
