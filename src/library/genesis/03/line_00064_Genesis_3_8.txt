Interpretive Reflections
-This is the first act of withdrawal. The Divine’s resonance hasn’t changed—but the humans’ capacity to remain open has. They no longer exist in naked transparency. Now, even Divine approach feels confrontational. The phrase “from the face/presence of the Divine” (mi’pnei) shows that what was once connection is now evasion. The voice, once life-giving, becomes overwhelming. They choose concealment within the very creation they were meant to cultivate. This is the relational wound—not death yet, but distance.
