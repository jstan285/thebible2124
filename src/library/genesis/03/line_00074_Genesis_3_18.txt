Interpretive Reflections
-This is the ground’s new language. It still responds to the human, but now speaks in resistance and distortion. “Thorns and thistles” are not just plants—they are metaphors of energetic fragmentation: sharp, entangled, unintended. The human will no longer feed from the fruit of aligned trees (Genesis 2:9), but from the low-yield herb of the outer field. Eden was a harmonized loop; this is asymmetric effort-output. The world still provides—but now under strain and scarcity logic, reflecting the internal shift.
