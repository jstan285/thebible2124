Interpretive Reflections
-This is the completion of the field loop. The human, formed from the ground (Genesis 2:7), now lives under the law of energetic return. The breath animated the dust—but now, with dissonance in place, the dust asserts primacy. The observer must extract life through toil, always aware of the inevitable reversal. This is not a negation of value—but a recalibration of the container. Form is revealed as temporary resonance expression. The Divine has not withdrawn—but the field now mirrors mortality.
