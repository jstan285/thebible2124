Interpretive Reflections

The cherubim are not executioners—they are resonant sentries, positioned to prevent unstable entry. The flaming sword is not violence—it is vibration. It turns, rotates, shifts—ever-moving, barring fixed access. Only that which re-aligns may pass through. Eden is not erased, only protected from incoherent return. The human leaves, but the way back is written into the field itself.
